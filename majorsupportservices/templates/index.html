About Us

Empowering Lives, Enriching Futures

We are Major Support Services, your dedicated provider of disability support services, extending our assistance to all NDIS participants.Our mission is to create a nurturing environment for NDIS participants, where they receive comprehensive support to foster personal and professional development.

Our Mission

Our core mission is to craft and provide secure, inclusive, and cutting-edge solutions tailored to our clients. We employ a client-centric methodology to reach predefined objectives, consistently aiming for the highest levels of satisfaction.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a beacon of inspiration in the disability support industry, where every individual's potential is nurtured, their dreams are realized, and their path to a brighter future is illuminated by our unwavering commitment to care, inclusivity, and innovation.

Core Values of Major Support Services

We work to bring smiles, hope, and a brighter future to those in need.

Empathy

We approach our work with deep understanding and compassion, putting ourselves in the shoes of those we serve to provide the best possible support.

Inclusivity

We celebrate diversity in all its forms, valuing and respecting every individual's unique background, culture, and beliefs.

Empowerment

We empower our clients to take control of their lives, fostering independence, confidence, and self-determination.

Quality

We are committed to delivering the highest standards of care and support, continuously striving for excellence in all that we do.

Teamwork

Collaboration and cooperation are at the heart of our success, as we work together with our clients, their families, and our colleagues to achieve positive outcomes.

Innovation

We embrace creativity and innovation to find new and effective ways to enhance the lives of those we serve, staying at the forefront of the industry.
